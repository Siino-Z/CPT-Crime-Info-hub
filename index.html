      Safety Tips

      • Be aware of your surroundings at all times.
      • Avoid walking alone at night in high-risk areas.
      • Report suspicious activity to SAPS: 10111
      • Save emergency contacts on your phone.
      • Share alerts with friends and family.

      To report a crime or emergency, call 10111 or visit your nearest police station.
